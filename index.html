About

Process + values

My Experience

I’ve spent 10 years at Google across San Francisco, New York, and now Atlanta. I build learning experiences the way architects build spaces: clear intent, strong structure, and measured pacing. Each project starts with a single objective, then moves through disciplined systems that balance clarity, tone, and performance.

My Approach

I work closely with stakeholders to translate complex ideas into clean, scalable learning systems. I use AI to accelerate development and iterate quickly, but every decision is grounded in human judgment and subject-matter context. The result is focused, modern design that feels confident and usable without excess.

Capabilities

Learning strategy, curriculum design, microlearning systems, video, LMS enablement.

Outcomes

Shorter ramp time, higher completion, and clearer handoffs through reusable systems.

Principles

Clarity over cleverness, momentum over bloat, and design that earns trust.
