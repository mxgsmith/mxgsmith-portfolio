About Me

My Experience

I design learning that’s intentional, structured, and built to perform. FAQ

Quick answers
What services do you provide?

I design and build learning experiences that scale — from e-learning and training videos to structured knowledge bases and consulting support. Sometimes that means hands-on production. Other times it means creating systems, templates, or guidance so teams can move faster and more consistently.

Do you use AI in your work?

Yes — as a tool, not a replacement. I use AI to speed up development, explore options, and reduce busywork, while human judgment drives the structure, tone, and final decisions. The goal isn’t novelty — it’s quality, consistency, and speed.

What kinds of problems do you help teams solve?

I help teams bring clarity to messy, fast-moving situations — especially when learning content needs to be built quickly, kept consistent, or scaled across audiences. If things feel fragmented, unclear, or stuck, that’s usually where I’m most useful.
